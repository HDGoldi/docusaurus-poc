## Status

| Parameter     | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| :------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `Activated`   | Green checkmark, indicating that full SIM functionality is activated and the SIM can connect to the 1NCE services.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| `Deactivated` | Black cross, indicating that the SIM is deactivated. The SIM cannot connect to the 1NCE network.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| `Expired`     | The `Expired` status indicates that the SIM card has reached the end of its lifetime and is no longer active. This means the SIM is fully suspended in the network and cannot connect anymore. Using a  can reactivate the SIM.  |
