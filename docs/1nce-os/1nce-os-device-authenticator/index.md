## Device Authenticator

The Device Authenticator offers a secure and automatic onboarding service for devices. The Device Authenticator is based on the Sim-as-an-Identity principle. Through unique identifiers, each SIM Card is securely authenticated and can be immediately used to send data to the [device integrator](doc:1nce-os-device-integrator).

## Sim-as-an-Identity

The `ICCID` of the customer SIM is used as a unique identifier for the device.
